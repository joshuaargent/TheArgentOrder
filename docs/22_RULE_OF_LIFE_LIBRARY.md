# The Argent Order

## Rule Of Life Library

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Formation System

---

# Purpose

A Rule of Life is not a habit tracker.

A Rule of Life is not self-improvement.

A Rule of Life is a deliberate structure for Christian living.

The purpose is to order a man's life toward God.

Everything else follows from that.

---

# Foundational Principle

A man cannot rely on motivation.

A man must rely on structure.

The Rule Of Life provides that structure.

---

# The Five Pillars

Every Rule Of Life in The Argent Order is built around:

## Faith

Relationship with God.

---

## Discipline

Mastery over self.

---

## Brotherhood

Responsibility to others.

---

## Building

Meaningful work and creation.

---

## Truth

Alignment with reality.
