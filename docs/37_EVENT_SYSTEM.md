# The Argent Order

## Event + Call System

Version: 2.1

Status: Canonical Hybrid Formation Infrastructure

---

# Purpose

The Event System exists to make formation **real in human terms**, not just digital tracking.

Men do not change through dashboards.

Men change through:

* shared struggle
* accountability under pressure
* real-time conversation
* embodied presence when possible

But the system must also function when physical presence is impossible.

Therefore:

```text id="purpose"
Formation must survive distance, time zones, and life constraints
```

---

# Core Principle

```text id="core"
Presence is ideal.
Participation is required.
Accountability is non-negotiable.
```

---

# 2. SYNCHRONOUS FORMATION LAYER (CALL SYSTEM)

This replaces the idea that “events are the only real connection point.”

Calls are the **primary human reinforcement system**.

---

## CALL TYPES

---

## A. POD CALLS (CORE WEEKLY STRUCTURE)

Frequency:

* Weekly (minimum)

Format:

* Voice or video
* Structured agenda
* Mandatory where possible

---

### Pod Call Structure

#### 1. Opening (5 min)

* Reminder of mission
* Alignment check

---

#### 2. Check-In Round (15–25 min)

Each member reports:

* Wins (objective actions)
* Failures (no excuses framing)
* Current focus (next 7 days)

---

#### 3. Accountability Review (20–30 min)

Measured against:

* Campaign progress
* Rule of Life adherence
* Discipline consistency
* Brotherhood participation

---

#### 4. Direct Feedback Phase (10–20 min)

* Peer correction
* Leader correction
* No avoidance allowed
* No vague statements allowed

---

#### 5. Commitment Lock (5–10 min)

Each member states:

```text id="commitment"
Exact actions I will complete before next call
```

No motivational language. Only commitments.

---

## B. 1:1 ACCOUNTABILITY CALLS

Purpose:

* intervention
* support
* discipline correction
* personal alignment

---

Used when:

* member is struggling
* member is inconsistent
* member is at risk of dropping out
* member requests support

---

Structure:

* reality check
* review of behaviour
* correction plan
* next commitment

---

## C. LEADERSHIP CALLS

Participants:

* Captains
* Officers
* Mentors

---

Purpose:

* pod health review
* system adjustments
* leadership calibration
* problem escalation

---

Structure:

* pod reports
* failure analysis
* leadership feedback
* system improvements

---

## D. STRATEGIC CALLS (STEWARD LEVEL)

Purpose:

* long-term direction
* system evolution
* doctrine protection
* structural decisions

# HYBRID PARTICIPATION MODEL

A key principle:

```text id="hybrid"
Distance does not reduce status or participation quality
```

If a member cannot attend physically:

They participate via:

* live call access
* structured reflection input
* accountability submission

---

No downgrade in:

* leadership status
* formation score
* participation value

---

# ASYNC PARTICIPATION SYSTEM

If live attendance is impossible:

Member must submit:

* written check-in
* campaign update
* failures + corrections
* next commitments

---

This ensures:

```text id="async"
No missed accountability cycles
```
