# Core Philosophy

Most creators optimize for attention.

The Order optimizes for conviction.

We are not trying to reach everyone.

We are trying to reach the right men.

Content should repel the wrong audience.

Content should attract the right audience.

A smaller audience of committed men is more valuable than a massive audience of spectators.

---

# Content Mission

Every piece of content must do at least one of four things.

Teach

Challenge

Inspire

Guide

If it does none of these:

Do not publish it.
