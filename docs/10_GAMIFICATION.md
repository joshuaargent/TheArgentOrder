# The Argent Order
## Gamification System

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The purpose of gamification is reinforcement.

Not addiction.

Not entertainment.

Not vanity.

Most gamification systems reward activity.

The Argent Order rewards formation.

The objective is not to make men chase points.

The objective is to make virtuous actions visible, measurable, and repeatable.

The game serves formation.

Formation never serves the game.

---

# Core Principle

Every reward should reinforce one of the Five Pillars.

Faith

Discipline

Brotherhood

Building

Truth

If a mechanic does not strengthen one of these pillars, it should not exist.

# Ultimate Principle

The purpose of gamification is not to make men addicted.

The purpose of gamification is to make growth visible.

The highest achievement in The Argent Order is not a badge.

Not a score.

Not a rank.

The highest achievement is becoming a faithful Catholic man who serves God, his family, his parish, and his community well.

Everything else is merely a tool that supports that mission.
