# The Argent Order

## Rule Of Life Library

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Formation System

---

# Purpose

A Rule of Life is not a habit tracker.

A Rule of Life is not self-improvement.

A Rule of Life is a deliberate structure for Christian living.

The purpose is to order a man's life toward God.

Everything else follows from that.

---

# Foundational Principle

A man cannot rely on motivation.

A man must rely on structure.

The Rule Of Life provides that structure.

---

Every Rule Of Life in The Argent Order is built around:

## Faith

---

## Discipline

Mastery over self.

---

## Brotherhood

Responsibility to others.

---

## Building

Meaningful work and creation.

---

## Truth

Alignment with reality.

# CAMPAIGN LIBRARY

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Formation Curriculum

---

## Purpose

Campaigns are structured transformation systems. They are controlled environments designed to force change over time.

A Rule of Life is stability. A Campaign is pressure. Together they form transformation.

---

## Core Principle

A man does not change by insight. A man changes by structured repetition under accountability.

---

## Campaign Structure

Every campaign contains: Objective, Duration, Daily Tasks, Weekly Milestones, Rules, Restrictions, Accountability System, Completion Criteria, Reward

---

## FOUNDATION CAMPAIGN

Duration: 21 Days

Daily Tasks: Morning Prayer, Scripture, Rule Of Life Completion, 1 Hour Deep Work, Daily Reflection

Milestone: No missed day for 21 days

Reward: Initiate → Brother promotion eligibility, Formation boost unlocked

---

## PRAYER CAMPAIGN

Duration: 30 Days

Daily Tasks: 15 minutes mental prayer, Rosary (as assigned), Scripture reading, Sunday Mass attendance

Restrictions: No skipping without reflection log

Reward: Faith score multiplier, Prayer streak badge

---

## DISCIPLINE CAMPAIGN

Duration: 30 Days

Daily Tasks: Wake at fixed time, Exercise, Cold shower, No social media before 9am, Deep work block (2–4 hours)

Milestone: 90% compliance rate

Reward: Discipline rank increase

---

## BROTHERHOOD CAMPAIGN

Duration: 28 Days

Daily Tasks: Pod check-in, Weekly meeting attendance, Accountability message

Weekly Milestones: 1 meaningful conversation per week minimum

Reward: Pod leadership eligibility

---

## BUILDING CAMPAIGN

Duration: 30 Days

Daily Tasks: 3 hours deep work, Project progress update, Ship something weekly

Milestone: MVP or working project by end

Reward: Builder Hall recognition

---

## TRUTH CAMPAIGN

Duration: 14 Days

Daily Tasks: Daily Examen, Reflection entry, Identify avoidance behavior

Weekly Milestone: Honest audit submission

Reward: Access to advanced formation track

---

## CHASTITY CAMPAIGN

Duration: 21–30 Days

Daily Tasks: Digital discipline rules, No sexual content exposure, Replacement habits (exercise, prayer)

Rules: Failure requires reset of streak

Reward: Discipline multiplier, Leadership eligibility boost

---

## LEADERSHIP CAMPAIGN

Duration: 30 Days

Daily Tasks: Pod oversight activity, Accountability enforcement, Weekly report submission

Milestones: Manage group outcome successfully

Reward: Captain eligibility

---

## MARRIAGE PREPARATION CAMPAIGN

Duration: 60 Days

Daily Tasks: Prayer discipline, Emotional regulation practice, Responsibility tracking, Communication exercises

Reward: Advanced formation unlock

---

## FATHERHOOD CAMPAIGN

Duration: 90 Days

Daily Tasks: Long-term planning exercise, Discipline consistency, Leadership journaling

Milestones: Structured life plan

Reward: Stewardship certification eligibility

---

## BUILDER CAMPAIGN

Duration: Ongoing (90 day cycles)

Daily Tasks: Ship code/product, Update progress, Iterate weekly

Milestone: Public launch

Reward: Builder Certification eligibility

---

## ADVANCED CAMPAIGNS

* Vocation Discernment Campaign - Align life with calling
* Saintly Discipline Campaign - Extreme formation intensity
* Steward Track - Leadership of leaders

---

## CAMPAIGN SYSTEM RULES

Rule 1: No passive participation. Either complete or fail.

Rule 2: Failure is recorded. No silent resets.

Rule 3: All completion must be verified: Journal entry, Pod confirmation, Bot verification

Rule 4: Campaigns always generate: Formation events, Analytics events, Achievement checks

---

## PORTAL/DISCORD INTEGRATION

Campaign UI must show: Daily task list, Progress bar, Failure state, Pod accountability status

Bot must: Remind daily tasks, Track completion, Announce completion, Enforce accountability

---

## Final Principle

Campaigns are pressure systems that exist to force transformation through repetition, accountability, and structured struggle.

# CERTIFICATION LIBRARY

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Certification & Progression Framework

---

## Purpose

Certifications exist to recognize demonstrated competence, reliability, and service. They are evidence that a man has consistently demonstrated a particular standard.

---

## Core Principles

* Responsibility before recognition. A title is earned through action.
* Character before competence. Skill without virtue is insufficient.
* Certifications unlock opportunities to serve, not to boast.
* Certifications can be revoked for failure to maintain standards.

---

## Progression Framework

### Rank Track (Trust and Responsibility)

Visitor → Initiate → Brother → Veteran → Captain → Officer → Mentor → Steward

### Certification Track (Demonstrated Capability)

Formation, Builder, Brotherhood, Leadership, Mentorship, Specialist

A man may hold multiple certifications.

---

## RANK SYSTEM

### Initiate

Entry Rank. Requirements: Joined community, Accepted rules, Completed onboarding, Connected Discord and Portal

### Brother

First earned rank. Requirements: Foundation Campaign completed, Rule Of Life established, Minimum activity period, Pod participation

### Veteran

Reliable member. Requirements: Multiple campaigns completed, Sustained activity, Positive community contribution

### Captain

First leadership rank. Requirements: Brotherhood Certification, Leadership Certification, Proven accountability

### Officer

Community operations leader. Requirements: Long-term reliability, Leadership track completion, Steward recommendation

### Mentor

Formation leader. Requirements: Mentor Certification, Demonstrated maturity, Leadership approval

### Steward

Highest operational rank. Requirements: Exceptional character, Proven leadership, Long-term service

---

## CERTIFICATION SYSTEM

### FORMATION CERTIFICATION

Demonstrate foundational Catholic formation.

Requirements: Foundation Campaign, Prayer Campaign, Active Rule Of Life, Weekly reviews completed

Unlocks: Advanced campaigns

---

### DISCIPLINE CERTIFICATION

Demonstrate personal consistency.

Requirements: Discipline Campaign, Streak requirements, Rule compliance benchmarks

Unlocks: Leadership pathway

---

### BROTHERHOOD CERTIFICATION

Demonstrate accountability and service.

Requirements: Pod participation, Meeting attendance, Peer endorsements

Unlocks: Captain pathway

---

### BUILDER CERTIFICATION

Recognize productive creation (Developers, Entrepreneurs, Creators, Builders)

Requirements: Active project, Public launch, Project documentation

Unlocks: Builder Hall recognition

---

### LEADERSHIP CERTIFICATION

Demonstrate ability to lead responsibly.

Requirements: Brotherhood Certification, Leadership Campaign, Team management experience

Unlocks: Captain eligibility

---

### MENTOR CERTIFICATION

Prepare men to guide others.

Requirements: Long-term consistency, Positive reputation, Leadership recommendation

Assessment: Mentorship practicum, Interview

Unlocks: Mentor rank eligibility

---

### POD LEADER CERTIFICATION

Train pod leaders.

Requirements: Brotherhood Certification, Pod leadership experience

Assessment: Attendance metrics, Pod health metrics

---

### PROJECT LEADER CERTIFICATION

Lead builder initiatives.

Requirements: Builder Certification, Team project completion

---

### EVANGELIZATION CERTIFICATION

Communicate Catholic truth effectively.

Requirements: Formation Certification, Content contribution, Faith discussions

---

### STEWARDSHIP CERTIFICATION

Prepare future Stewards.

Requirements: Officer or Mentor level service, Long-term reliability, Leadership recommendation

Assessment: Steward council review

Unlocks: Steward eligibility

---

## CERTIFICATION LEVELS

Every certification can have levels: Builder I/II/III/Master Builder, Leadership I/II/III

This creates progression without unnecessary rank inflation.

---

## RECERTIFICATION

Some certifications require renewal (Mentor, Leadership, Pod Leader): 12 Month renewal period

---

## DIGITAL BADGES

Portal displays: Earned certifications, Levels, Date awarded

Discord Roles mirror certification status.

---

## DISCORD INTEGRATION

Bot commands: /certifications, /certification-progress, /certification-status

---

## Final Principle

Ranks indicate trust. Certifications indicate competence. Neither exists for status. Both exist to support formation, service, responsibility, and mission.

The highest achievement in The Argent Order is becoming a man who can be trusted with responsibility and who consistently serves God, family, community, and society.
