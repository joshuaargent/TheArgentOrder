# Purpose

Growth is not the mission.

Growth is the consequence of fulfilling the mission.

The objective is not to become large.

The objective is to become effective.

If growth compromises doctrine, culture, brotherhood, or formation, growth should be rejected.

The Order seeks sustainable expansion without cultural dilution.

The question is not:

"How do we get more members?"

The question is:

"How do we find more men who need formation?"
