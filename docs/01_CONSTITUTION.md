# The Argent Order
## Constitution

Version: 1.0

---

# Preamble

The Argent Order exists to help men become who God created them to be.

We believe modern men are suffering from a crisis of formation.

Many men are isolated.
Many men are distracted.
Many men lack purpose.
Many men lack discipline.
Many men lack meaningful brotherhood.

The Argent Order exists to address these problems through Catholic formation, disciplined action, structured accountability, and authentic brotherhood.

This Constitution serves as the governing document of The Argent Order.

All leadership decisions, platform features, moderation actions, campaigns, and community initiatives must align with the principles outlined herein.

---

# Article I
# Authority

The ultimate authority of The Argent Order is:

1. God
2. Sacred Scripture
3. Sacred Tradition
4. The Magisterium of the Catholic Church

The Order is explicitly Catholic.

The Order is not non-denominational.

The Order is not generic spirituality.

The Order is not secular self-improvement.

The Order is not political.

The Order exists to help men live authentic Catholic lives.

---

# Article II
# Core Values

Every member is expected to pursue these values.

---

## Faith

Faith is the foundation.

Without faith, all other achievements become disordered.

Faith must influence:

- Prayer
- Work
- Relationships
- Leadership
- Decision making

---

## Discipline

Discipline is obedience to what is right despite what is easy.

The Order values:

- Consistency
- Responsibility
- Self-control
- Sacrifice

Excuses are rejected.

Ownership is encouraged.

---

## Brotherhood

Brotherhood is essential.

Isolation weakens men.

Brotherhood strengthens men.

Members are expected to:

- Encourage one another
- Challenge one another
- Support one another

Brotherhood is not optional.

---

## Building

Men are called to create.

Building includes:

- Businesses
- Software
- Skills
- Careers
- Families
- Communities

Consumption alone is insufficient.

Members are encouraged to become builders.

---

## Truth

Truth exists independent of feelings.

Reality matters.

Evidence matters.

Logic matters.

Truth must be pursued with charity.

---

# Article III
# Member Expectations

Every member is expected to:

- Pursue holiness
- Seek truth
- Take responsibility
- Act with integrity
- Treat others charitably
- Participate honestly

Members are not expected to be perfect.

Members are expected to be serious.

---

# Article IV
# Conduct Standards

The following behaviors are prohibited:

- Harassment
- Doxxing
- Pornography
- Malicious deception
- Spam
- Predatory behavior
- Threats
- Criminal activity

Immediate removal may occur.

---

# Article V
# Culture Standards

The Order rejects:

- Victim mentality
- Chronic excuses
- Persistent cynicism
- Learned helplessness
- Passive consumption

The Order encourages:

- Responsibility
- Courage
- Initiative
- Service
- Excellence

---

# Article VI
# Debate Standards

Debate is permitted.

Debate is encouraged.

Truth is sharpened through discussion.

Debate must follow these principles:

- Attack ideas
- Never attack people
- Assume good faith
- Provide evidence
- Seek understanding
- Remain charitable

Winning arguments is secondary.

Discovering truth is primary.

---

# Article VII
# Brotherhood Standards

Members should actively participate in brotherhood.

Brotherhood includes:

- Accountability
- Encouragement
- Mentorship
- Friendship
- Service

Every member should eventually belong to an accountability pod.

---

# Article VIII
# Leadership Philosophy

Leadership is service.

Leadership is not status.

Leadership is responsibility.

The purpose of leadership is:

- Protect culture
- Guide members
- Encourage growth
- Resolve conflict
- Serve the mission

Leadership exists for the good of the community.

---

# Article IX
# Leadership Structure

The Order uses the following progression.

Visitor

↓

Initiate

↓

Brother

↓

Veteran

↓

Captain

↓

Officer

↓

Mentor

↓

Steward

---

# Visitor

Exploring the Order.

No responsibilities.

---

# Initiate

New member.

Learning culture.

Beginning formation.

---

# Brother

Committed participant.

Active in campaigns.

Active in accountability.

---

# Veteran

Demonstrates consistency.

Contributes positively.

Trusted member.

---

# Captain

Leads pods.

Supports brothers.

Models standards.

---

# Officer

Protects culture.

Moderates discussions.

Handles disputes.

Supports operations.

---

# Mentor

Forms future leaders.

Provides guidance.

Represents the culture.

---

# Steward

Responsible for long-term direction.

Protects the mission.

Protects the vision.

---

# Article X
# Accountability

Accountability is central to formation.

Members are encouraged to:

- Set goals
- Review progress
- Admit failures
- Pursue improvement

Accountability exists for growth.

Not shame.

Not humiliation.

Not status.

---

# Article XI
# Work Philosophy

Work is participation in creation.

Work is honorable.

Work is service.

Members are encouraged to:

- Develop skills
- Build projects
- Pursue excellence
- Create value

The Order strongly supports:

- Developers
- Entrepreneurs
- Students
- Professionals
- Tradesmen
- Creators

---

# Article XII
# Technology Philosophy

Technology serves formation.

Technology must never become the goal.

The website serves acquisition.

The newsletter serves retention.

Discord serves brotherhood.

The portal serves formation.

Every tool should answer:

"Does this help form stronger Catholic men?"

If not, it should not be built.

---

# Article XIII
# Growth Philosophy

Growth is welcomed.

Growth is not worshipped.

The Order prioritizes:

1. Transformation
2. Culture
3. Formation
4. Growth

In that order.

Growth that damages culture is failure.

---

# Article XIV
# Success

Success is measured by transformation.

The Order succeeds when members:

- Grow in faith
- Build discipline
- Form friendships
- Develop competence
- Serve others

Numerical growth alone is insufficient.

---

# Article XV
# The Final Principle

The purpose of The Argent Order is not popularity.

The purpose of The Argent Order is not influence.

The purpose of The Argent Order is not wealth.

The purpose of The Argent Order is to help men become who God created them to be.

All decisions must ultimately serve this objective.
