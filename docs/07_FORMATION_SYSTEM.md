# GAMIFICATION SYSTEM

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Behaviour Reinforcement System

---

## Purpose

Gamification exists to reinforce discipline, consistency, and responsibility.

Not entertainment.

Not addiction.

Not dopamine loops.

The system exists to make correct behaviour the easiest behaviour to continue.

---

## Core Principle

The purpose of gamification is reinforcement.

Not addiction.

Not entertainment.

Not vanity.

Most gamification systems reward activity.

The Argent Order rewards formation.

The objective is not to make men chase points.

The objective is to make virtuous actions visible, measurable, and repeatable.

The game serves formation.

Formation never serves the game.

---

## Virtue Principle

Gamification must reward virtue, not attention.

If a system rewards noise, it produces noise.

If it rewards discipline, it produces disciplined men.

---

## Primary Loop

```
Action

↓

Tracking

↓

Feedback

↓

Progress

↓

Increased Responsibility
```

---

## Core Metrics System

The entire system is built around 5 core metrics.

---

### 1. Faith Score

Measures consistency in spiritual practice.

Includes:

* Prayer completion
* Scripture reading
* Mass attendance
* Rosary practice

---

### 2. Discipline Score

Measures self-control and execution.

Includes:

* Wake time consistency
* Exercise completion
* Habit adherence
* Digital discipline

---

### 3. Brotherhood Score

Measures engagement with community.

Includes:

* Pod attendance
* Check-ins
* Accountability responses
* Support given to others

---

### 4. Builder Score

Measures output and creation.

Includes:

* Projects shipped
* Work sessions completed
* Deliverables created
* Progress logs

---

### 5. Truth Score

Measures honesty and self-awareness.

Includes:

* Daily reflection completion
* Failure logging
* Weekly review honesty
* Avoidance detection

---

## SCORING PRINCIPLE

Scores are not for comparison.

They are for clarity.

---

Bad usage:

* Ranking men against each other
* Ego inflation
* Competitive superiority

---

Correct usage:

* Identifying weakness
* Tracking growth
* Guiding correction
