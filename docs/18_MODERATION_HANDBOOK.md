# The Argent Order

## Moderation Handbook

Version: 2.0

Status: Canonical Community Governance System

---

# Purpose

Moderation exists to protect:

* Catholic Orthodoxy
* Brotherhood
* Formation
* Safety
* Mission

Moderation is not punishment.

Moderation is stewardship.

The purpose is not to remove people.

The purpose is to maintain an environment where men can grow.

---

# Foundational Principle

Protect the mission.

Protect the brothers.

Protect the truth.

In that order.

---

# Moderator Philosophy

Moderators are not police.

Moderators are custodians.

Their role is to:

* Maintain standards
* Correct errors
* Resolve conflicts
* Protect members
* Preserve culture

# Moderation Ladder

Every case starts at the lowest appropriate level.

---

## Level 1

Informal Correction

Purpose:

Redirect behaviour.

Example:

```text id="l1"
Brother, let's move this discussion back on topic.
```

---

## Level 2

Formal Warning

Recorded.

Purpose:

Document behaviour.

---

## Level 3

Restricted Access

Temporary.

Examples:

* Muted channels
* Read-only access
* Campaign removal

---

## Level 4

Suspension

Temporary removal.

Typical duration:

```text id="l4"
7-30 Days
```

---

## Level 5

Permanent Ban

Reserved for serious violations.

---

# Members In Distress

Possible indicators:

* Extreme hopelessness
* Withdrawal
* Crisis language
* Self-destructive statements

---

Moderator Actions

1. Move discussion private.

2. Notify leadership.

3. Encourage real-world support.

4. If imminent risk appears present, provide crisis resources and encourage contacting emergency services or trusted local adults/family.

5. Document actions.

---

The community does not provide therapy.
