# The Argent Order
## Roles & Permissions

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines:

- Authority
- Responsibility
- Leadership
- Promotions
- Permissions
- Governance

The purpose of rank within The Argent Order is not status.

The purpose of rank is responsibility.

The higher a man's rank, the greater his obligation to serve.

Leadership is earned.

Leadership is not granted based on popularity.

Leadership is not granted based on seniority alone.

Leadership is granted based upon:

- Character
- Competence
- Contribution
- Consistency
- Trust

---

# Leadership Philosophy

Leadership exists to:

- Protect the Mission
- Protect the Culture
- Protect the Brotherhood
- Protect the Members

Every leader is expected to:

- Lead by example
- Serve before being served
- Accept responsibility
- Remain teachable
- Remain accountable

No leader is above correction.

No leader is above the Constitution.

---

# Authority Structure

The formation ranks within The Argent Order proceed as follows:

Steward

↓

Mentor

↓

Officer

↓

Captain

↓

Veteran

↓

Brother

↓

Initiate

↓

Visitor

---

**Note on "Founder":** The "Founder" is not a formation rank. The Founder is the originator of the Order who operates outside the formation hierarchy. The Founder's role is described in the Founder Playbook (see 29_FOUNDER_PLAYBOOK.md). The Founder's evolution follows the path: Builder → Operator → Architect → Steward. At the Steward level, the Founder becomes indistinguishable from other Stewards in terms of rank and authority within the formation system.

# FOUNDER

**Note:** The "Founder" is not a formation rank within The Argent Order. The Founder exists outside the formation hierarchy as described in 35_FOUNDER_PLAYBOOK.md.

## Purpose

Guardian of the mission.

The Founder is responsible for:

- Vision
- Direction
- Major decisions

The Founder remains accountable to:

- Catholic teaching
- The Constitution
- The Mission

**Founder Evolution:** The Founder's role follows the path: Builder → Operator → Architect → Steward. Upon reaching Steward level, the Founder's role becomes indistinguishable from other Stewards in terms of rank and authority within the formation system.
