# The Argent Order

## Analytics System

Version: 1.0

Status: Canonical Measurement Framework

---

# Purpose

You cannot improve what you cannot measure.

But you also cannot measure everything.

This system focuses only on metrics that reflect real transformation.

Not vanity metrics.

Not engagement theatre.

Not superficial activity.

Only meaningful change in men.

---

# Core Principle

Analytics exist to answer one question:

```text id="core"
Are men actually being transformed?
```

Everything else is secondary.

---

# SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

The analytics system is split into 5 layers:

```text id="layers"
Acquisition

Activation

Engagement

Formation

Leadership
```

---

# 1. ACQUISITION METRICS

## Purpose

Measure entry into the ecosystem.

---

### Metrics

* Landing page conversion rate
* Email signups
* Discord joins
* Source attribution (YouTube, X, referrals)

---

### Key Insight

High acquisition means nothing without activation.

---

# 2. ACTIVATION METRICS

## Purpose

Measure whether new members take first action.

---

### Metrics

* Roll-call completion rate
* Rule of Life creation rate
* First campaign start rate
* First week participation rate

---

### Critical Threshold

If activation < 40%, system is failing onboarding clarity.

---

# 3. ENGAGEMENT METRICS

## Purpose

Measure consistency of participation.

---

### Metrics

* Daily check-in completion rate
* Campaign task completion rate
* Discord active participation rate
* Portal daily logins

---

### Interpretation Rule

Engagement must be tied to structure, not entertainment.

---

# 4. FORMATION METRICS

## Purpose

Measure actual transformation.

This is the most important layer.

---

### Metrics

* Campaign completion rate
* Rule of Life adherence score
* Streak stability (not peak streaks)
* Failure recovery rate
* Weekly review completion rate

---

### Key Insight

A man who fails but recovers is better than a man who avoids failure entirely.

---

# 5. LEADERSHIP METRICS

## Purpose

Measure multiplication of leadership.

---

### Metrics

* Number of Captains active
* Pod stability rate
* Pod leader retention
* Mentor-to-member ratio
* Leadership promotion rate

---

### Critical Insight

If leadership does not grow, the system collapses under scale.

---

# POD HEALTH ANALYTICS

Each pod has a health score:

---

## Inputs

* Attendance rate
* Campaign completion rate
* Communication frequency
* Leader engagement
* Member consistency

---

## Pod States

```text id="pods"
Healthy

Stable

At Risk

Failing
```

---

## Intervention Rules

* At Risk → Monitor + support
* Failing → restructure or dissolve

---

# INDIVIDUAL FORMATION SCORE

Each member has a Formation Score composed of:

---

## Components

* Faith consistency
* Discipline consistency
* Brotherhood participation
* Builder output
* Truth honesty

---

## Important Rule

This score is private.

Only visible to:

* User
* Assigned leader
* System

---

No public ranking system is allowed.

---

# RETENTION ANALYTICS

## Purpose

Measure long-term stability.

---

### Metrics

* 7-day retention
* 30-day retention
* Campaign re-entry rate
* Drop-off points per journey stage

---

### Key Insight

Retention drops indicate structural failure, not user failure.

---

# TRANSFORMATION METRICS

## Purpose

Measure real-world change.

This is the highest priority layer.

---

### Metrics

* Testimonial submissions
* Project launches
* Leadership promotions
* Habit permanence (90+ day streaks)
* Reported life improvements

---

### Interpretation

If transformation metrics are low:

System is producing activity, not change.

---

# LEADERSHIP PIPELINE ANALYTICS

## Purpose

Measure leadership sustainability.

---

### Metrics

* Brother → Veteran conversion rate
* Veteran → Captain conversion rate
* Captain retention rate
* Mentor availability rate
* Steward pipeline strength

---

### Critical Insight

Weak pipeline = future collapse.

---

# GROWTH QUALITY INDEX (GQI)

A composite metric:

---

## Formula

```text id="gqi"
GQI = Transformation Rate × Retention Quality × Leadership Growth
```

---

## Interpretation

* High growth, low transformation = failure
* Low growth, high transformation = acceptable
* High both = optimal system

---

# ALERT SYSTEM

System triggers alerts when:

---

## Red Flags

* Drop in campaign completion > 20%
* Pod failure rate increases
* Leadership churn increases
* Engagement without formation increase

---

## Response

* Investigate system design
* Adjust onboarding or campaigns
* Strengthen pods
* Review leadership

Analytics must never become:

* Entertainment dashboards
* Vanity scoreboards
* Competitive leaderboards

---

They exist only for:

* Correction
* Improvement
* Formation integrity

---

# FINAL PRINCIPLE

If analytics do not lead to better men, they are useless.

If analytics improve discipline, truth, and formation, they are essential.

Measurement exists to ensure the system remains aligned with its mission:

Form Catholic men who live with discipline, truth, brotherhood, and purpose.
