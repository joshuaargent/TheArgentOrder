# The Argent Order
## Brotherhood System

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Brotherhood is the retention engine of The Argent Order.

Content attracts.

Campaigns transform.

Brotherhood retains.

A man may join because of content.

He stays because of brotherhood.

The purpose of this system is to ensure no committed member walks alone.

Every member should eventually know:

- Who he is accountable to
- Who is accountable to him
- Who can support him
- Who he can support

The Order rejects anonymous participation.

Brotherhood requires relationship.

---

# Brotherhood Philosophy

Modern men suffer from isolation.

Many men have:

- Acquaintances
- Followers
- Online contacts

But very few brothers.

The Argent Order seeks to restore authentic male brotherhood.

Brotherhood means:

Mutual responsibility.

Mutual encouragement.

Mutual correction.

Mutual support.

Mutual growth.
