# The Argent Order
## Formation System

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Formation is the core product of The Argent Order.

Discord is not the product.

Content is not the product.

The Portal is not the product.

Formation is the product.

Everything exists to produce transformation.

The objective is not to create informed men.

The objective is to create formed men.

---

# Definition Of Formation

Formation is the process through which a man becomes who God created him to be.

Formation is not information.

Formation is not inspiration.

Formation is not motivation.

Formation is:

Truth

↓

Action

↓

Habit

↓

Character

↓

Identity

↓

Vocation

↓

Holiness
