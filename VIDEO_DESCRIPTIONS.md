## Key Principles for All Descriptions

1. **Lead with the identity:** ⚔️ Catholic men. Formed in discipline.
2. **State the problem:** Most men are stuck/drift/trying harder/isolated
3. **Present pods as the core:** "Pods of 5-8 men who meet weekly"
4. **Explain the daily mechanic:** Roll call (Prayer? Workout? Work?)
5. **Explain the weekly mechanic:** Pod meeting (60 min, structured)
6. **Differentiate clearly:** "Not a community. A forge."
7. **Include the 5 pillars:** Faith ⚡ Discipline ⚡ Brotherhood ⚡ Building ⚡ Truth
8. **Key phrase:** "The system doesn't change men. Pods change men."
9. **One CTA:** theargentorder.vercel.app (everywhere)
10. **End with hashtags:** #Catholic #Masculinity #Formation #Pods (and relevant pillar tags)
